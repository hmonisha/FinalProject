Your Streaming Guide ...

The most disappointing aspect of this entire process is not being able to find a proper website to watch the show. It's such a headache!

One page will tell you to pay for the content while another will have weird, random ads on every corner of the screen that will say "Jessica, 25 miles away wants to meet you!", which is DISGUSTING! and everytime you click on the "x" to exit out of the stupid ad, it opens another tab!

Anyway, my friends, let me make this easier for you. Scroll down to find the least creepiest website and apps I've come across that can make the process of watching your favorite Kdrama more enjoyable and less annoying! (yes, they have subs and good quality ;) )

F R E E

Rakuten Viki

This app is known as the most famous website that explicitly streams drama shows from the range of countries. It is free of download for Android and IOS. Users can find kdramas with different categories, year, etc. Movies and other content is also available. Downside: Too many ads but bearable for the free service!

NewAsianTV

The website is considered as the hub of Korean, Japanese and Asian dramas. Users can search for their serials by different genres, year, country, etc. The website is easy to use with none to very minimal ads.

TDrama.Net

A free streaming site for movies and shows that is easy to navigate and use. Users can search for shows or movies through different categories, such as country or name of the content. Users are also able to download the content to their device. None to very minimal ads.

DramaCool

Users can watch and download Korean drama, movies, and shows to their device. Users can search for content by selecting a type of dramas, korean shows, or movies by language or the most popular.

AsianCrush

Users can browse movies and dramas of different languages by using the country, movie, and show category. Website is easy to navigate. No ads.

Dramanice

Users can watch movies and shows. They can categorize by language and the letters of the alphabet to easily locate the name of a drama/movie. Easy to navigate, no ads.

P A I D

MyDramaList

This paid website allows users to watch top/popular movies and tv shows by paying for it. There is also a community for users to chat and share their experiences/opinions.

OnDemand Korea

Users can watch premium korean dramas from past seasons or on-air, along with content for kids, documentaries, sports, education, etc.

Netflix

Netflix is diverse when it comes to its content, so of course it is one of the options. It has enough good-rated kdramas that can convince a non-kdrama watcher to start watching kdramas. convincing enough? great. It's only $10.00 a month if you can afford it.

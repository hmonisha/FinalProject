안녕하십니까 (Hello)

and welcome to the safe space! I'm so happy to see you here. My name is Monisha and I created this website to share my love for Korean Dramas! This is for anyone looking into Kdrama recommendations or anyone who wants to explore! :) Use the buttons in the top nav to look around. <3

I love watching KDramas because...

Romance

Unlike the western television series, Korean creators treat romance scenes with so much importance and respect. Every kiss, hug, and touch is used sparingly, giving them the spotlight they deserve while keeping the audience on their feet!

Comedy

Kdramas are INSTANT mood changers! They are capable of easily brightening your day. Every genre is filled with funny moments that are sure to give you a good laugh. Quirky individuals, charming sense of humor and their never-seen stupid comic facials are sure to make you addicted! Unlike western content, men in Kdramas aren't afraid to show their full depths of emotions!

Fashion/Cast

All the actors and actresses are top-tier gorgeous! They are all so effortlessly beautiful that I can't help but stare and get lost in their time-less beauty!
South Korea is one of the most important fashion capitals in the East and is an irresistible market for brands! We are able to see of this through the fashion in Kdramas portrayed by the wonderful actors and actresses

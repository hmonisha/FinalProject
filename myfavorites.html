And...

One of the gifts a Kdrama addict can give another is the title of a
wonderful show they have not yet discovered. Here are some of my
few recommendations and appreciations of Korean dramas from the
distant past to the recent past, all of shows that I consider worthy of
being called "great." - Monisha
Strong Woman Do-Bong Soon

Do Bong-soon is a woman born with superhuman strength that comes from the long line of women possessing it. when Ahn Min Hyuk, the CEO of ainsoft, a gaming company witnesses her strength he hires her as his personal bodyguard. She desperately wants to become a willowy and elegant woman, which is the ideal type of her crush, In Gook-du (Ji Soo), a police officer. One day, a series of kidnapping cases break in Dobong-dong, the district Bong-soon lives in, and she is determined to catch the culprit after her friend was targeted.

Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-Joo

This drama revolves around Kim Bok joo (Lee Sung-Kyung), a young woman chasing her dream of weightlifting on an athlete college campus and Jung Joon-Hyung, an athletic swimmer on their college campus team. Kim Bok joo develops a crush on her friend Jung Joon-Hyung's (Nam Joo-Hyuk) older brother, Jung Jae-Yi (Lee Jae-Yoon). Joon-Hyung goes along with Bok Joo's act of making his brother fall in love with her until Joon-Hyung finds himself falling for Bok-Joo. This is a coming of age story that revolves around the college life of Bok-Joo and Joon-Hyung fighting for their dreams while finding love in the process.

While You Were Sleeping

Hong-Joo lives with her mother and helps her run a restaurant. Hong-Joo is haunted by seeing the future deaths of others in her dreams. What's worse is that she does not know when the deaths will happen, but she tries to stop her dreams from becoming reality. Jae-Chan (Lee Jong-Suk) is a rookie prosecutor who moves to new house with his younger brother, and they become neighbors with Hong-Joo. Jae-Chan dreams of Hong-Joo meeting with an accident and when he makes an attempt to save her and admit about his dream, they work together to solve crime cases.

W

A romance takes place between Kang Chul, who is super rich and exist in the Webtoon “W,” and Oh Yeon-Joo, a surgical resident in the real world who is pulled into the is pulled into the Webtoon world of W, which was created by her father, and becomes entangled in a murder mystery.

Suspicious Partner

Noh Ji Wook is a brainiac prosecutor and Eun Bong Hee is a rookie justice department intern, who always seems to find herself in the most unfortunate and embarrassing situations. One day, Bong Hee is falsely accused of murdering her ex-boyfriend, and Ji Wook gets demoted because he risks his career and reputation to save Bong Hee. They must catch the real culprit before time runs out and they get themselves killed. It becomes complicated when Bon Hee and Ji Wook’s careers and love life become entangled.

Legend of the Blue Sea

The series centres on the love story of Shim Cheong, a mermaid, and Heo Joon-jae, a witty con-artist. Focusing on rebirth, fate, and unrequited love, their tale is juxtaposed with the parallel story of their Joseon-era incarnations, the mermaid, Se-hwa, and town head, Kim Dam-ryeong.
